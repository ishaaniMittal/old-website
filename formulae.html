Formula-E : User Experience Challenge

Mobile app to increase the user engagement on the race day.

Prototyping
UX Design
Prototyping
UX Design

Motivation

Design an immersive experience for the spectators/fans of Formula-E during the race and guide them to numerous activities. Which informs race-goers of essential race-day information. In a nutshell, it means that the app should not only extend its usage and purpose within the racetrack but also before and after the race. Using an ‘App’ instead of a Computer kiosk would allow constant access by the user throughout their experience.

Design

Research done

We conducted online surveys, in person interviews, desktop research along with competitive analysis of other similar ‘Wayfinding’ apps. We asked questions relating to their habits and preferences not only around the racetrack but also before and after. The questionnaire can be viewed here.


Insights from Research

Having 54 responses to our Survey through Google Forms provided us meaningful insights and helped us come up with a feature list. Lot of unexpected insights emerged from the research such as need to better comprehend live race positions that a user saw from his stand view. Another unexpected insight was realizing the importance to user of showing all relevant track information.


Feature List

After going through the insights, we had rounds to come up with feature lists of our own without being influenced by the other team member. Once we came up with our individual lists, we had a session where we combined and discarded a few ideas. The final feature list is as below.

    1) MAP NAVIGATION : An interactive map that shows the location of all stalls/areas and guides the user towards various areas. It would also have a comprehensive list of all events and stands that are there on the race day.
      a. Map
        i. 2D Map with Markers
          1. Rating of the place
      b. Weather
        i. Temp
        ii. Precipitation Probability
        iii. Humidity
      c. Track Conditions
        i. Track info (Laps, Circuit Length, Race Distance)
        ii. Moisture Air
        iii. Friction
        iv. Track Temperature
      d. Events + Activities
      e. Food ordering
        i. List of Eateries
          1. Current rating of the place
        ii. Menu of eateries
        iii. Order now
        iv. Track the order progress page
    2) LIVE RACE : This feature would show the live stats of team timings and race leaders and also show live race feed so that the user isn’t missing any action. It would also have an AR function. This would allow a user to use the AR camera feature which would show what team just passed their stand.
      a. Live Video
        i. Show 4 dummy segments on the screen
      b. Live Stats
        i. Lap Times
        ii. Current Standing
        iii. Fanboost
      c. AR car notification
        i. Team Name
        ii. Driver
        iii. Position
        iv. Car Info
    3) RACE FEED : This feature would show the live stats of team timings and race leaders and also show live race feed so that the user isn’t missing any action. It would also have an AR function. This would allow a user to use the AR camera feature which would show what team just passed their stand.
      a. Formula E Series Stats
        i. Player Profile with live steering wheel info
      b. News stories
      c. About Formula E
    4) PROFILE :
      a. E-Ticket : Having a digital ticket would allow the user to travel hassle free and easier without any need to print it. This ticket would also be hyperlinked to the map, so that the user can see where their seat is, in which stand.
        i. Name
        i. Seat Number
      b. E Wallet : We intend to introduce and ePayment and eWallet system for easier purchase of items at the venue.
        i. Purchase History
      c. Social Media : This would provide a user the means to add customizable race frames, stickers around their pictures as well as a platform to share their activities via facebook and twitter. Having this integration into the app itself would help generate an even bigger 'online buzz' around the track events.
        i. Frames & Photos
        ii. Twitter & Facebook
    5) RECOMMENDATIONS FOR FUTURE EVENTS : This would provide a user the means to add customizable race frames, stickers around their pictures as well as a platform to share their activities via facebook and twitter. Having this integration into the app itself would help generate an even bigger 'online buzz' around the track events.
    6) FAN BOOST VIBRATION : As a team uses the ‘Fan Boost’, the app would make the user’s phone vibrate and send an audio tone. This would increase the user engagement and help foster greater interest and excitement into the race.
