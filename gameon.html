Game On!

Game On provides a comprehensive list of live matches being telecasted by various sports bar and restuarants across the city.

Prototyping
Mobile App
Prototyping
Mobile App

Motivation

The aim is to create a holistic platform for sports lover. A one stop solution for all their sporting needs, be it looking for a restuarant or a bar to watch a match or looking for a company to play a match or looking for a place to play a match. The project is being done in a team of two. I am responsible for creating the front end of the application.

Design

The initial app screens looked like the ones shown above. As the app started to become more complicated with more features being added, it became very time consuming to create interfaces directly in android. After spending some time on the search engine, I learned about two very effective techniques to create wireframes and mock-ups.

First I created paper mock-ups of the interface. After the paper wireframes were finalized, we wanted to get the feeling of the application without coding anything in android. This required for us to create interactive mock-ups. This need made me explore Proto.io, a very effective interactive mock-up tool and the interactive mock- ups were created using the same. Now the designs have been finalized and we have moved on to create interfaces in android.
