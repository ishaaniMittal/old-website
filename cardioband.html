Motivation

Cardio band was conceptualized as a part of the Summer Intern done at National Informatics Center, India. The main motivation behind this device was an aim to provide timely help to patients who get sudden heart stroke. Studies show that many deaths are caused because of help not being available on time. Cardio band bridges this gap by alarming the concerned surroundings on time and providing a timely help. Cardio Band is my brain child. The technical work was done by me and the documentation and market research was done by the other two members.

Prototype

The prototype contains an arduino uno board as the heart of the device. A GSM module; containing a mobile sim;  is attached to the arduino in order to send isms to the numbers pre fed in the system. In order to simulate the heart rate, a pulse sensor module was used. Visualization of the heart rate was carried out using Processing.
